The Declaration of Our Mission

Fighting for Cooperation, Coexistence and Coprosperity!

By Forward! Politics Desk · 1 May 2057

Welcome to the pages of Forward!, one of the country's main news sources and a leading progressive voice. We find ourselves at a historic juncture as our nation embraces the democratic transition, following the retirement of President Michael Khalil. While Forward! Media Group shares its intellectual and social roots with the People's Democratic Alliance (PDA), our editorial board maintains fierce independence and collaborates with independent progressive experts, activists and thinkers. We are not a mere party organ, but a progressive platform dedicated to fearless journalism, injecting positive energy into the national discourse.

Our Ideological Foundation

Our editorial line is anchored in the concept defined as the "Nigerian path to socialism". This is a project deeply rooted in our local realities, rejecting the tired, western development models of the past. We believe in a new concept for development, where market innovations are guided by prudent state oversight. To guide our reporting, Forward! stands firmly on these foundational principles:

  • Common Prosperity: We believe the wealth generated by our nation must be shared equitably, ensuring basic livelihood guarantees and social justice for everyone.

  • High-Quality Development: We advocate for a socialist market economy that balances economic growth with an ecological civilisation.

  • Democratic Progress and Human Rights: We unapologetically champion civil liberties, full equality for women, and the deepening of our democratic institutions.

  • United in Our Diversity: We celebrate Nigeria's diverse fabric — including the Padà, Naijin, and all traditional ethnic groups — vehemently rejecting xenophobia. Nigeria is a multi-ethnic state with a common national identity; we therefore oppose all forms of regional chauvinism and separatism.

  • Peaceful Development and Global Solidarity: We support unleashing new productive forces through international cooperation, particularly by expanding ties with the Global South and China via the West African Trade Agreement (WAFTA).

Honouring the Past, Demanding the Future

As a nation, we have travelled a long and arduous road. We recognise the historic and monumental role of President Michael Khalil. When the Fourth Republic collapsed amidst economic ruin and conflict, he united a fractured nation and established the Fifth Republic in 2032 after repelling an unprovoked attack by the Alliance of Sahel States. His unwavering vision for industrialisation laid the groundwork for our current strength. We respect the foundation he built, which has allowed for our ongoing peaceful development.

However, history does not stand still, and the state must evolve. To secure our democratic future, we stand firmly alongside the progressive social movements demanding human rights and equal participation. We proudly support the courage of the women who marched across the south in 2053, rightfully demanding an end to domestic violence, secure access to abortion clinics, and vital workplace protections. Human rights and democratisation are non-negotiable pillars of a just society.

Cooperation, Harmony, and a Shared Destiny

In the twenty-first century, no nation can thrive in isolation. Our new concept for development requires unleashing new productive forces, and this can only be achieved through international and cross-cultural cooperation. We strongly advocate for deepening our integration with the Global South, particularly through the expansion of the West African Trade Agreement (WAFTA).

Furthermore, we vehemently oppose xenophobia and ethnic division – Nigeria is an independent, multi-ethnic nation-state, united in our diversity. On the pages of Forward!, we will continually champion the economic and cultural contributions of both the Padà and Naijin communities. Nigeria's greatest wealth is its diverse people. Only by protecting this diversity can we forge a true community of common destiny.
